## Objective

The objective of this assignment was to define a clear and honest proof statement by identifying one primary claim, one target audience, and one desired action. Instead of using AI to generate the answer directly, I used Claude as a thinking partner to refine my ideas through an interview-based conversation.

## Files Included

- **Proof-Statement.pdf** – Final proof statement and one-line explanation.
- **Claude-Interview.pdf** – Complete interview conversation with Claude showing the iterative process used to refine the proof statement.

## What I Learned

This assignment helped me understand the difference between making broad claims and identifying a specific, truthful statement that my portfolio can support. It also showed me the importance of using AI to challenge my thinking rather than simply generate answers.

## Outcome

Created a focused proof statement that reflects my current learning journey and career direction while maintaining honesty about my skills and experience.
